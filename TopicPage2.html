2.1 Factors affect photosynthesis The main factors affecting rate of photosynthesis are light intensity, carbon dioxide concentration and temperature.

2.2 Product of photosynthesis The main product of photosynthesis is glucose, which is the molecule that produces energy to run the processes of the cell.

2.3 Importance of photosynthesis Green plants and trees use photosynthesis to make food from sunlight, carbon dioxide and water in the atmosphere
